Three Decades of Quality and Trust

Siva Constructions | A Legacy of Excellence

ISO 9001-2012 Certified Company, established in 1992. For over three decades, Siva Constructions has earned a reputation for reliability and high-quality work. During this time, the organization has successfully completed more than 75 projects, including both commercial and residential developments in prime locations.

We truly believe that homes are among the most emotional and significant purchases people make in their lifetimes. We are deeply passionate about our work and feel a profound responsibility to ensure that the experience of owning and living in our homes brings joy and pride. We extend this philosophy to every inch of the two million square feet we have built so far.

Since its inception in 1992, Siva Constructions has continuously striven to enhance its homes and customer experience through ongoing interactions with our over two thousand clients, understanding their needs and aspirations.

Over the past 30+ years, we have consistently incorporated the insights derived from every new project we undertake, leading us to develop several landmark projects in the Guntur and Vijayawada areas. Each one of our 75+ projects reflects our vision to create homes where you can truly build a good life.

FOUNDER

Our Visionary Founder

Shri Alla Siva Reddy, M.com

Siva Constructions was founded by Mr. Alla Siva Reddy three decades ago. Sri Alla Siva Reddy is one of the most respected figures in the construction industry. He is a builder with a clear vision and unshakable integrity. While there are many leaders with good ideas, very few can actually implement those ideas and make a significant impact. He is one of the rare individuals who can, and the projects he has executed have made an enormous difference in the quality of life for many people.

With his extensive experience in construction, he has not only provided affordable homes but also fundamentally changed lifestyles through his prestigious gated community projects in the heritage cities of Amaravati and Guntur. He has held leadership and senior advisory roles in various industry bodies, including the Confederation of Real Estate Developers’ Associations of India (CREDAI).His commitment to excellence and bespoke ambitious projects stands as a testament to his courage. He has been recognized on multiple national platforms, including receiving the TV5 Business Leader Award.

Homes are the most emotional and important purchases that people make in their lifetimes. So, we should make them more responsible. Alla Siva Reddy, Founder.
MANAGING DIRECTOR

Leadership with Integrity

Shree Nagarjuna Reddy Alla, B.Tech (Civil), M.B.A

Mr. Nagarjuna Reddy Alla holds a bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering from Acharya Nagarjuna University and a postgraduate degree in Construction Management from the National Institute of Construction Management & Research, Pune.

He is passionate about emerging technologies and their application in the construction field to achieve the goal of sustainable development. As Managing Director, he has played a pivotal role in completing prestigious projects undertaken by Siva Constructions at affordable prices for customers. Although he is the son of the founder, his work style and collaboration with employees are a testament to his commitment. His approach to engaging with clients and customers is a craft in itself.

He has been an active member and has held responsible officer positions in CREDAI (Confederation of Real Estate Developers' Associations of India) at both the Guntur and Andhra Pradesh levels. CREDAI focuses on promoting real estate development in an ethical manner while protecting the interests of all stakeholders.
